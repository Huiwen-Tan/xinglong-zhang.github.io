Research Interests


I am interested in chemical reactions occurring both in a laboratory setting as well as in a biological system. In particular, I am interested in the underlying reaction mechanisms since such an understanding will allow us to better design and develop catalysts for chemical syntheses. The advent of better computational resources allow us to investigate chemical reactions and mechanisms in silico, allowing the acceleration of catalyst screening. I will be using and developing new computational tools to achieve better catalyst design for my DPhil study under Prof. Robert Paton.

I am also interested in the development of quantum mechanical methods that can be applied to the investigation of real chemical systems.
